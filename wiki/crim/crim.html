Column Row Impartial Merge (CRIM)

Integer Parition Combinatorial Game

CRIM (Column Row Impartial Merge) is played on a Young diagram. On your turn, remove any whole row or whole column. If this splits the shape into separate pieces, they are immediately joined back into one. This is an impartial game — the player who makes the final move (for normal play) wins.

Moves

Following the description above, the moves work as below:

  • (a) Column removal
  • (b) Row removal
